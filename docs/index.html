Rhonda Glazier Wins Beacon Award

New Clarivate Leadership

Yoel Goldenberg, new SVP & GM of Library Software Solutions, addressed the conference.

Goldenberg joins from the enterprise AI space — previously CPO at Jacada (conversational AI & automation) and SVP Product Management at Uniphore (AI agents & conversational analytics).
